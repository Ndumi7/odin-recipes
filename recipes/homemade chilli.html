Homemade Chilli

Description

This homemade chili is loaded with beef, beans, and crowd-pleasing flavor and won't take up much of your time to prepare.

    Ingredients
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 (15 ounce) can tomato sauce
  • 1 (15 ounce) can kidney beans
  • 1 (14.5 ounce) can stewed tomatoes
  • 1 Â½ cups water, or as needed (Optional)
  • 1 pinch chili powder, or more to taste
  • 1 pinch garlic powder
  • salt and pepper to taste
    Steps
  1. Place ground beef and onion in a large saucepan over medium heat; cook and stir until meat is browned and onion is tender, about 5 to 7 minutes.
  2. Stir in tomato sauce, kidney beans, and stewed tomatoes with juice. If you prefer a thinner consistency, you can add water.
  3. Season with chili powder, garlic powder, salt, and black pepper.
  4. Bring to a boil, reduce heat to low, cover and let simmer for 15 minutes.
